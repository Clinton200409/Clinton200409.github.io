Final Project!

This program is a more fulfulling version of a frogger game that we made a little while ago in this class.
The main menu has a single player, two player, and an instructions page.
When you click on a game mode you will have to choose between a variety of characters provided.
In the two player mode, you can choose a difficulty level. (Easy, Medium and Hard)
You must reach 20 points before the game is over to win and earn a congratulations screen.
And lastly, dont forget to have fun!!
